Character Dimensions:
The DE0-Nano has 22,300 LE's. In order to fit the whole design on the board the characters must be constranted to a size limit.

Resolution: 640x480
Char Width: 10
Char Hight: 16

640/10 = 64 Chars/Row
480/16 = 30 Rows
64x30 = 1920 Chars/Frame
1920x8 (bytes) = 15360 Registers | LE's
